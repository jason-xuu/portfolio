Project Description

The goal is to explore the advancements in machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL) technologies and identify opportunities for Minority Business Enterprises (MBEs) to leverage these innovations. Ensuring accessibility and scalability for MBEs can promote inclusivity, bridge the digital divide, and unlock untapped potential within underrepresented communities.

Machine learning and deep learning technologies have become central to technological advancements, offering numerous opportunities for businesses to gain a competitive edge. However, MBEs face challenges in accessing these technologies due to historical barriers. This project aims to bridge this gap and ensure that the benefits of ML and DL are accessible to all.

The Story

Recent trends in AI, ML, and DL present significant opportunities for MBEs. Generative models like ChatGPT and Google Bard excel in image synthesis and data generation, while transfer learning leverages pre-trained models to improve performance in new tasks. Autonomous vehicle technology is revolutionizing the automotive industry with advancements in computer vision and deep learning. The AI market is dominated by the US, China, and India, with the US leading in investments and initiatives to promote responsible AI research. The Biden Administration’s efforts to root out bias in AI systems and the National Science Foundation’s funding of new AI research institutes highlight the US’s commitment to AI leadership. To drive MBE participation, targeted programs providing access to resources, partnerships, training, and policy influence are essential. Proactive monitoring for biases and fostering an inclusive environment will empower underrepresented communities and promote diversity and innovation.
